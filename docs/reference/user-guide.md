## Getting Started

### Account Types

| Role | Description | Access |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Student** | Classes 6–12 learners | Study, quizzes, progress, gamification |
| **Teacher** | School teachers | Quiz creation, paper generation, student monitoring |
| **Parent** | Parents/guardians | View linked children's progress |
| **Admin** | School administrators | Full school, teacher, student, content management |

### First-Time Setup

1. Visit the AskAide AI platform URL
2. Click **Sign Up** (or accept an invite link from your school)
3. Select your role and complete registration
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile (class, subjects, school)

> **Students:** If your teacher shared a class code, enter it during signup to auto-link to your school and teacher.

> **Teachers/Admins:** You typically receive an invite link from your school admin. Use that link to register with your school pre-linked.

## Student Guide

### Student Login & First Steps

**Logging In:**
1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Login**

**First-Time Onboarding:**
1. Select your **class** (6–12)
2. Choose your **subjects** (e.g., Mathematics, Science, English)
3. Enter your **school name** (or skip if not applicable)
4. Set a **daily study goal** (recommended: 30 minutes)
5. You'll be redirected to your student dashboard

### Study Sessions

Study sessions are the core learning experience. You practice MCQ and fill-in-the-blank questions tailored to your chapter and difficulty level.

#### Starting a Study Session

1. From the sidebar, click **Study Sessions** (or **Start Studying** from the dashboard)
2. **Step 1 — Select Class:** Choose your class level (6–12)
3. **Step 2 — Select Subject:** Pick a subject (e.g., Mathematics)
4. **Step 3 — Select Chapter:** Browse chapters within the subject and pick one (e.g., "Number Systems")
5. **Step 4 — Select Difficulty:**
   - **Easy** — Fundamentals and basics
   - **Medium** — Application-level questions
   - **Hard** — Advanced and competitive exam-level questions
6. Click **Start Session**

> **PLACEHOLDER: `[Screenshot of study session setup — class → subject → chapter → difficulty]`**

#### During a Study Session

- Questions appear one at a time
- **MCQ:** Select the correct option from A/B/C/D
- **Fill-in-the-blank:** Type your answer in the text field
- After answering, you'll see:
  - ✅ Correct / ❌ Incorrect indicator
  - **Explanation** for the correct answer
  - **Related topic** link for deeper study
- Click **Next** to proceed to the next question

#### Session Summary

At the end of each session:
- Total questions attempted
- Accuracy percentage
- Time taken
- Breakdown by topic/difficulty
- Points earned

### Student Quizzes

Quizzes assigned by your teachers appear here.

#### Viewing Assigned Quizzes

1. Click **Quizzes** in the sidebar
2. You'll see a list of quizzes with status:
   - 🟢 **Available** — Ready to attempt
   - 🟡 **In Progress** — Started but not submitted
   - 🔴 **Upcoming** — Not yet released by teacher
   - ⚪ **Completed** — Already attempted

> **PLACEHOLDER: `[Screenshot of quiz list with status badges]`**

#### Taking a Quiz

1. Click on an available quiz
2. Read the instructions (time limit, number of questions, marking scheme)
3. Click **Start Quiz**
4. Answer questions — navigate freely between questions
5. Click **Submit** when done (or it auto-submits when the timer runs out)

#### Viewing Quiz Results

After submission:
- Score and percentage
- Correct/incorrect breakdown
- Time taken vs. time allowed
- Question-by-question review with explanations

#### Quiz History

- Access all past quizzes under the **History** tab

- Review any past quiz in detail

### Gamification

AskAide AI keeps you motivated through gamification features.

#### Daily Streaks

- Study every day to build your streak
- Each consecutive day adds +1 to your streak
- Missing a day resets the streak (unless you use a **Streak Freeze**)

#### Streak Freezes

- Earned through milestones and achievements
- Protects your streak for one missed day
- View your available freezes in the dashboard

#### Badges

| Badge | How to Earn |
|-------|-------------|
| 🏅 **First Step** | Complete your first study session |
| 🔥 **7-Day Warrior** | Maintain a 7-day streak |
| 💯 **Perfect Score** | Get 100% accuracy in a session |
| 📚 **Bookworm** | Complete 50 sessions |
| 🏆 **Subject Master** | Master all topics in a subject |
| 🎯 **Sharpshooter** | Attempt 500+ questions |
| ⭐ **Challenge Champion** | Complete 30 daily challenges |
| 🤝 **Team Player** | Refer 3 friends |

#### Leaderboards

- **Weekly leaderboard** — Ranked by points earned in the current week
- **All-time leaderboard** — Ranked by total points
- Filter by class, school, or subject

#### Daily Goals

- Set a daily study goal (e.g., 30 minutes, 20 questions)
- Track progress toward your goal on the dashboard
- Earn bonus points for meeting your goal consistently

#### Referrals

1. Go to **Profile → Referrals**
2. Share your referral code/link with friends
3. When a friend signs up and completes their first session, you both earn bonus points

### Quiz Management

#### Creating a Quiz

1. Click **Quizzes → Create New Quiz**
2. Fill in quiz details:
   - **Title** (e.g., "Chapter 3 — Algebra Test")
   - **Class & Subject**
   - **Time Limit** (in minutes)
   - **Total Marks**
   - **Instructions** (optional)
3. Add questions using one of these methods:

**Option A — From Question Bank:**
1. Click **Add from Question Bank**
2. Filter by chapter, topic, difficulty
3. Select questions (checkboxes)
4. Click **Add Selected**

**Option B — Custom Questions:**
1. Click **Add Custom Question**
2. Choose type: MCQ or Fill-in-the-blank
3. Enter question text, options/correct answer, and explanation
4. Repeat for each question

4. **Reorder questions** by dragging (optional)
5. Click **Save as Draft** or **Publish**

> **PLACEHOLDER: `[Screenshot of quiz creation form]`**

#### Publishing a Quiz

- **Draft** → **Published** makes it visible to students
- Set an **available from** and **deadline** date/time
- Students see it in their quiz list once published

#### Quiz Analytics

After students attempt the quiz:
1. Click **Analytics** on the quiz card
2. View:
   - Average score, highest/lowest scores
   - Per-question accuracy (% who got it right)
   - Time distribution
   - Student-by-student breakdown

#### Cloning a Quiz

- Click the **Clone** icon on any existing quiz
- A copy is created as a draft
- Modify as needed and publish for another class

### AI Assistant

Use natural language to generate educational content.

1. Click **AI Assistant** in the sidebar
2. Type a prompt, for example:
   - *"Generate a 20-question MCQ quiz on Class 10 Physics Chapter 2 — Light"*
   - *"Create a practice paper for Class 8 Mathematics, Chapters 1–3, 50 marks"*
   - *"Write notes on Class 12 Chemistry — Organic Reactions"*
   - *"Make a homework assignment on Class 9 Biology — Tissues"*
   - *"Generate a worksheet with 15 fill-in-the-blank questions on Class 7 History — Medieval India"*
3. The AI generates content
4. **Review** and **edit** as needed
5. **Save** to your question bank or **publish** directly

|------|-------------|
| **Quiz** | MCQ and fill-in-the-blank questions |
| **Paper** | Full exam paper with marking scheme |
| **Notes** | Chapter/topic summary notes |
| **Assignment** | Homework tasks with instructions |
| **Worksheet** | Practice sheets for classroom use |

## Admin Guide

### School Management

Admins manage the entire school's data on the platform.

#### Creating a School

1. Click **Admin Panel → Schools**
2. Click **Add School**
3. Fill in:
   - School name
   - Address
   - Contact email & phone
   - Board (CBSE / ICSE / State)
4. Click **Save**

#### Editing a School

1. Click on the school name in the list
2. Edit fields as needed
3. Click **Update**

#### Deleting a School

1. Click the **Delete** icon next to the school
2. Confirm the deletion
3. All linked teachers, students, and sections are unlinked (not deleted)

### Chapter Upload

Upload PDF textbooks for AI processing (RAG, embeddings, question generation).

#### Uploading a Chapter PDF

1. Click **Admin Panel → Chapter Upload**
2. Select **Subject** and **Class**
3. Click **Choose File** and select a PDF
4. Enter:
   - Chapter name
   - Chapter number (optional)
5. Click **Upload**
6. The PDF is sent to the AI service for:
   - Text extraction
   - Chunking and embedding
   - Indexing in the vector database
7. Processing status appears in the list:
   - 🟡 **Processing** — Being analyzed by AI
   - 🟢 **Ready** — Available for quizzes and paper generation
   - 🔴 **Failed** — Check error message and retry

> **PLACEHOLDER: `[Screenshot of chapter upload page]`**

#### Bulk Upload

1. Select multiple PDFs
2. Assign class/subject to each
3. Upload all at once
4. Monitor processing status in the list

### Chapter Management

Manage chapters and their topics after upload.

#### Viewing Chapters

1. Click **Admin Panel → Chapters**
2. Browse by **Class** and **Subject**
3. View:
   - Chapter name and status
   - Number of topics extracted
   - **Question count** per chapter (red when `0`, green when questions exist)
   - Upload date
   - Processing status

#### Generating Questions

- Each chapter has a **Generate Questions** button that triggers AI question generation in the background (fire-and-forget).
- The button is **disabled until the chapter has topics** — upload and ingest the chapter PDF first.
- After triggering, refresh to watch the question count grow as questions are generated.

#### Editing Chapters

- Click a chapter to:
  - Rename it
  - Edit topic names
  - Delete individual topics
  - Re-upload the PDF (re-process)

#### Deleting Chapters

- Click **Delete** on a chapter
- Confirm deletion
- The associated vector embeddings are removed from the AI service

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| **Can't login** | Check email/password, reset password via "Forgot Password" |
| **No quizzes showing** | Ensure your teacher has published the quiz and assigned it to your class |
| **Chapter not processing** | Check admin panel for error status; re-upload the PDF |
| **Slow page load** | Clear browser cache, check internet connection |
| **Parent can't see child** | Verify the invite code was entered correctly; check link status |
| **AI generates wrong content** | Edit the generated content before publishing; provide more specific prompts |
| **Quiz results not showing** | Ensure quiz is submitted (not just saved); check analytics tab |
| **Streak not updating** | Streak requires completing at least one question; login alone doesn't count |
