### 3. Top 7 Strategic Gaps to Fill

| Priority | Gap | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | **Socratic AI Tutoring** | AskAide AI's RAG-based Q&A is not a true tutoring experience. Vidhya AI, Edzy, Obi, Fermi.ai, and Khanmigo guide students through problems rather than giving answers. This is the #1 feature requested in school AI pilots. Implementing a multi-turn Socratic dialogue agent would close the largest experience gap. |
| **2** | **Parent-Teacher Communication** | Schools purchasing B2B licenses expect parent engagement features. AskAide AI now has a Parent Dashboard (multi-child, real-time progress, streaks, activity feed), but parent-teacher communication (messaging, meeting scheduling) is still missing. Byju's, Super Tutor, and Obi offer parent dashboards but no competitor offers direct parent-teacher messaging тАФ a blue ocean opportunity. |
| **3** | **Knowledge Graph + GraphRAG** | Moving from document-level RAG to concept-level GraphRAG. Obi's 50K-node graph with 15-hop tracing and Fermi.ai's concept graph set a new bar for true adaptivity. Without concept-level prerequisite mapping, AskAide AI's "adaptive" practice is essentially keyword matching. This is the foundational investment that unlocks Socratic tutoring, misconception detection, and adaptive learning paths. |
| **4** | **Multi-Language Support** | Indian K12 education happens in 22+ regional languages. Obi (12+) and Khan Academy (50+) have demonstrated broad language support is feasible. AskAide AI's English-only interface excludes the majority of the target market. This is critical for B2B school adoption outside metro cities. |
| **5** | **Offline Support** | Many Indian schools have intermittent connectivity. PhysicsWallah, Byju's, and Doubtnut support offline access. Without it, AskAide AI is unusable in low-connectivity environments where the need for AI-powered learning tools is highest. |
| **6** | **LMS / Google Classroom Integration** | Schools already use Google Classroom and other LMS platforms. SparkSchool AI, QuestionWell, and AceQuiz offer direct integration. Without LMS sync, teachers face friction adopting AskAide AI alongside their existing workflow. |
| **7** | **Child Safety Compliance** | Growing parent concern about AI safety (86% want guardrails per EdWeek Mar 2026). SparkSchool leads with a dedicated safety page, FERPA/COPPA compliance, no student accounts model. VedaAI is DPDP Act 2023 compliant. AskAide AI has no public safety positioning тАФ a growing liability for school procurement decisions. |
