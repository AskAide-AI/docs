### 2.1 Referral Program (Already Built)

AskAide AI has a referral system. Maximize it:

| Tactic | Implementation | Impact |
|--------|----------------|--------|
| Referral reward | Free study time / practice sessions per referral | Medium |
| Teacher referral | Teachers get dashboard access for referring schools | High |

Users can share their practice session results as PNG images:

| Platform | Sharing Method | Viral Coefficient |

| WhatsApp | Share image directly | High (most used app in India) |
| Instagram Stories | Save + upload | Medium |
| Facebook / Facebook Stories | Direct share | Medium |
| Twitter / X | Share with hashtag | Low-Medium |

**Action:**
- Add "Share on WhatsApp" CTA with pre-filled text: "I just scored X% on my Class Y Science practice on AskAide AI! Try it free: [link]"

| Funnel Stage | Action | Conversion |
