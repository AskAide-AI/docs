# AI Research Prompt for AskAide AI

Copy and paste the following prompt into ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude to generate a deep market analysis for AskAide AI, specifically focusing on the Indian context.

***

**System Role:**
You are an expert Chief Product Officer and EdTech Market Researcher specializing in the Indian education sector (K-12). I am providing you with the **Product Manual** for a new B2B EdTech platform called **AskAide AI**.

**Input Data:**
*(Attach or Paste the content of the Product Manual here)*

**Your Task:**
Based on the product capabilities described in the manual, perform a deep research analysis on how this product will perform in the Indian market. Structure your response into the following sections:

## 1. Indian Market Fit & NEP 2020 Alignment
- **Curriculum Compliance:** How does the "Dynamic Question Generation" and "Topic Mastery" features align with the **CBSE/ICSE** board requirements and the Competency-Based Education (CBE) mandate of the **National Education Policy (NEP) 2020**?
- **Teacher Productivity:** How will the new "Automated Question Paper Generator" save time for Indian school teachers dealing with high workloads and examination pressures? How does the ability to instantly generate customized PDF exams via AI position AskAide against competitors?
- **Competitive Landscape:** Compare this "AI Adaptive Practice" approach against existing Indian giants (Byju's, Unacademy, PhysicsWallah) and B2B school solutions (Lead School, Next Education). Does this solve a problem they ignore?
- **USP Identification:** Identify specific features (e.g., Offline Detection, AI Tutoring) that give it a unique edge in the Indian market.

## 2. Technical & Infrastructure Viability in India
- **Connectivity:** Analyze the value of the "Offline Detection" feature for Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities in India where internet is intermittent.
- **Device Usage:** Evaluate availability considerations for low-cost Android devices common in Indian households.

## 3. B2B Sales Strategy for Indian Schools
- **Stakeholder Analysis:** Who is the decision maker? (Principal, Trustee, or Owner?)
- **The "Killer Pitch":** Craft a 1-minute pitch specifically for an Indian School Principal who is skeptical about "AI replacing teachers". Focus on *AskAide* as a "Teaching Assistant" not a "Teacher Replacement".
- **Lead Magnet Utilization:** The platform features a public-facing "Free Question Paper Generator" designed to capture leads (emails/phone numbers). How can we best utilize this tool in a Go-To-Market strategy targeting school principals and top-tier educators on platforms like LinkedIn or WhatsApp?
- **Pricing Strategy:** Suggest a pricing model suitable for "Affordable Private Schools" (APS) vs "Premium International Schools" in India.

## 4. Gap Analysis & Feature Recommendations
- **Vernacular Support:** Discuss the necessity of supporting Hindi and regional languages.
- **Exam Prep:** detailed the potential for adding JEE/NEET specific question banks.
- **Parental Loop:** How can the "Parent Dashboard" be optimized for Indian parents who are anxious about marks? (e.g., WhatsApp integration for reports).

## 5. Risk Assessment
- **Content Accuracy:** How critical is the accuracy of AI-generated questions in the rote-learning heavy context of Indian exams?
- **Regulatory:** Brief mention of the **Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act** implications for handling student data.

## 6. Pre-Pilot Feature Checklist (Go-to-Market Readiness)
- **MVP for Indian Schools:** Based on your analysis, provide a checklist of features that are absolute "must-haves" before pitching to a single school. What is a "deal-breaker" if missing? (e.g., Bulk Excel Uploads, PDF Report generation, specific Administrative locks).
- **Operational Requirements:** detailed any non-negotiable operational features (like data backup or printable logs) that school administrators in India typically demand.

## 7. Startup Launch & Operations Strategy
- **Business Structure:** Recommend the ideal legal structure for this B2B EdTech (e.g., Private Limited vs. LLP) considering future fundraising.
- **Funding Landscape:** What is the current investor sentiment for "AI in Education" in India? Identify potential grants (like Startup India) or incubators relevant to this product.
- **Go-to-Market (GTM) Plan:** Outline a 3-month launch plan. Should we target private chains, budget schools, or tuition centers first?
- **Team Composition:** Beyond engineers, what are the first non-technical hires needed? (e.g., B2B Sales Representative vs. Customer Success Manager).
- **Sales Cycle Expectations:** What is the typical duration from "First Meeting" to "PO Generaton" for Indian schools, and how can we shorten it?

## 8. The Ultimate Execution Roadmap (Synthesis)
- **Role-Based To-Do List:** Break down the immediate next steps into checklists for:
    - **The CEO (Business/Sales):** (e.g., "Register entity", "Print brochures").
    - **The CTO (Tech/Product):** (e.g., "Implement bulk upload", "Set up backup server").
- **Timeline:** Create a strict **Week-1 to Week-8** schedule to go from "Current State" to "First Pilot Signed". Be ruthless about prioritizing only what matters for the first sale.
